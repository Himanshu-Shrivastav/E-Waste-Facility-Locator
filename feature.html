About

Welcome to E-Waste Locator, your one-stop destination for responsible e-waste disposal and recycling. At E-Waste Locator, we are committed to promoting environmental sustainability and protecting human health by providing convenient solutions for the proper disposal of electronic waste (e-waste).

With the rapid advancement of technology, electronic devices have become an integral part of our daily lives. However, the improper disposal of these devices can have serious consequences for the environment and public health. Heavy metals, toxic chemicals, and other hazardous materials found in e-waste can leach into the soil and water, polluting our ecosystems and posing risks to human health.

At E-Waste Locator, we believe that everyone has a role to play in mitigating the impact of e-waste on our planet. Our platform offers a range of services to help individuals and businesses responsibly manage their electronic devices at the end of their lifecycle.

Our Mission:

  • Promoting Awareness: We aim to educate the public about the environmental and health impacts of e-waste and the importance of proper disposal practices.

  • Rewarding Sustainability: Through our innovative credit points system, users can earn rewards for responsibly disposing of their old electronic devices. By incentivizing sustainable behavior, we encourage individuals to take an active role in e-waste management.
